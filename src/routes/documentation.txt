================================================================================

  POST   /api/admin/login
         Authenticate an admin user with email and password.
         Returns a signed JWT token on success.
         Reads: admin

  POST   /api/admin/register
         Register a new admin account (name, email, password, title).

  PUT    /api/admin/change-email

  POST   /api/internal/job/newjob
         Create a new job with job number, company ID, and attention field.

         Affects: job

  POST   /api/internal/job/updateinvoiceandincrement

         global counter, and auto-assign the job to the current financial period if set.
         Affects: job, metadata, job_period

  GET    /api/internal/job/getjobs
         Get a paginated, sortable list of all jobs with company name. Supports attention filter.
         Reads: job, company

  GET    /api/internal/job/jobsummary
         Get a full job summary including job fields, company info, and linked parts with pricing.
         Reads: job, company, job_part, part

  GET    /api/internal/job/currentjobnum
         Get the current job number counter from the metadata store.
         Reads: metadata

  POST   /api/internal/job/updatejobnum
         Update the current job number counter in the metadata store.
         Affects: metadata

  POST   /api/internal/job/status
         Update the job status configuration JSON object in the metadata store.
         Affects: metadata

  POST   /api/internal/job/calculatecost
         Calculate subtotal and total_cost (with tax) for a job and save the result.
         Affects: job | Reads: job_part

  GET    /api/internal/job/getjobsbyclient
         Get a paginated list of jobs for a client (attention) name, each with associated parts.
         Reads: job, job_part, part

  GET    /api/internal/job/getjobsbycompany
         Get a paginated list of jobs filtered by company ID.
         Reads: job

  --- Job–Part Links ---

  POST   /api/internal/job/jobpartjoin
         Link a part to a job with quantity, price, revision, and details.
         Affects: job_part

  POST   /api/internal/job/updatejobpartjoin
         Update the quantity, price, revision, details, and note for a job-part link.
         Affects: job_part

  DELETE /api/internal/job/jobpartremove
         Remove a part from a job by deleting the job-part link.
         Affects: job_part

  --- Starred Jobs (Shop Floor Tracking) ---

  POST   /api/internal/job/starjob
         Star a job_part for active shop floor tracking, tagged to a client (attention).
         Affects: stars

  DELETE /api/internal/job/unstarjob
         Unstar a job_part and delete all associated tasks.
         Affects: stars, tasks

  GET    /api/internal/job/getstarredjobs
         Get all starred job_part IDs and their statuses (minimal payload).
         Reads: stars

  GET    /api/internal/job/getstarredjobsfull
         Get all starred job parts with full job, part, and company details.
         Reads: stars, job_part, part, job, company

  GET    /api/internal/job/getstarredjobsfilteredbyclient
         Get starred job_part IDs filtered by client (attention) name.
         Reads: stars

  GET    /api/internal/job/getstarredjobsfullbyclient
         Get full starred job details filtered by client (attention) name.
         Reads: stars, job_part, part, job, company

  GET    /api/internal/job/getstarredjobsfullbycompany
         Get full starred job details filtered by company ID.
         Reads: stars, job_part, part, job, company

  POST   /api/internal/job/getjobsbypartids
         Get full job and part details for a list of job_part IDs.
         Reads: job_part, part, job, company

  GET    /api/internal/job/checkstarred
         Check whether a specific job_part ID is currently starred.
         Reads: stars

  GET    /api/internal/job/checkjobstarred
         Check whether any part within a given job is currently starred.
         Reads: stars, job_part

  --- NFC Tag Pairing & Status Updates ---

  PUT    /api/internal/job/pairnfctag
         Pair an NFC tag ID to a starred job_part for scan-based status updates.
         Affects: stars

  PUT    /api/internal/job/unpairnfctag
         Clear the NFC tag association from a starred job_part.
         Affects: stars

  PUT    /api/internal/job/updatestarstatusbynfctag
         Update the production status of a starred job_part identified by its NFC tag.
         Affects: stars

  PUT    /api/internal/job/updatestarjobstatus
         Update the production status of a starred job_part by its job_part ID.
         Affects: stars

  PUT    /api/internal/job/updatestarstatusbyjobnumber
         Update the production status of a starred job_part by job number and part number.
         Affects: stars
